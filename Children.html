Lyndhurst Community Presbyterian Church

Children

Learn Creatively

Front Door: Ages 3 to 2nd grade - meet in Bloomfield Hall


The stories of the Bible capture the imagination and shape the beginning of faith. At LCPC we believe God is very much a part of who we are and is well at home in our heart. In this class children are invited to throw the door to their heart wide open and allow God to settle in.

Apes is an inventive name the kids created,an acronym meaning All People Enter Story. However, do not let the name fool you. This class openly enters into scripture to find personal meaning in the unfolding story of how the generations have experienced God.
